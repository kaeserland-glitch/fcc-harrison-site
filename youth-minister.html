The Opportunity

Youth Director Responsibilities

This is a part-time role with a full-time heart for ministry. The Youth Director provides spiritual, relational, organizational, and administrative leadership for our children and youth programs, working closely with volunteers, families, and church leadership.

  • Assist with a marketing plan to grow youth attendance
  • Provide spiritual, relational, organizational, and administrative leadership for children and youth programs
  • Instruct and nurture children and youth in their Christian faith and in life through Bible study, prayer, fellowship, worship, service, and evangelism
  • Foster a strong educational program by building relationships with volunteer teachers, students, parents, and other church leaders
  • Coordinate, recruit, and train volunteers to work in these programs
  • Develop programs and curriculum in conjunction with the Christian Education committee
