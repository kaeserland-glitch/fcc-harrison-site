About Us

A Community
Rooted in Grace

First Christian Church (Disciples of Christ) has been a home for seekers, believers, and servants in Harrison, Arkansas for generations. We are a non-creedal, open-communion congregation where every person is welcome at the Lord's table — no exceptions.

We believe in the freedom to follow Jesus together, shaped by scripture, guided by the Spirit, and committed to one another and our community.

  • Open communion — all are welcome at the Lord's Table
  • No creed but Christ, no book but the Bible
  • Baptism by immersion, though we accept all who follow Jesus
  • Committed to Christian unity and our neighbors in need
  • Part of the wider Disciples of Christ denomination

Ministries

Youth & Children's Ministry

We believe children and youth are the church NOW — not just its future. Every Sunday we create space for the next generation to grow in faith, friendship, and the love of God.

K – 5th Grade
Children's Sunday School
Our children's class meets every Sunday at 10:00 AM. Using age-appropriate Bible stories and activities, we help children discover their faith in a fun, welcoming environment. All-in-One Bible Fun curriculum.
6th – 12th Grade
Youth Ministry
Our youth meet every Sunday at 10:00 AM for meaningful discussion, community, and fun. We create space for middle and high schoolers to ask real questions and grow in faith together.
